Frequently Asked Question

What is UX design?

UX design stands for User Experience design. It is the process of designing digital or physical products that are easy to use, intuitive, and enjoyable for the user.

What are the key principles of UX design?

The key principles include user-centered design, accessibility, usability, and research-based decisions.

What is the difference between UX and UI design?

UX design focuses on the overall experience, while UI design focuses on the visual layout and interactive elements.

What is a wireframe?

A wireframe is a visual guide that represents the skeletal framework of a digital product.

What is user testing?

User testing involves evaluating a product by testing it with real users to gather feedback on usability.
